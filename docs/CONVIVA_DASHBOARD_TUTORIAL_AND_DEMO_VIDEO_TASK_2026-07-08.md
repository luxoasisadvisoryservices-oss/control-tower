# Conviva Dashboard Tutorial And Demo Video Task - 2026-07-08

## Decision

When Conviva is ready to launch or private-demo, create reusable videos so onboarding does not depend on repeated manual calls and explanations.

## Required Videos

1. Full dashboard tutorial
   - How to log in.
   - How to read the dashboard.
   - How to understand reservations, guest conversations, status, alerts and intervention flags.
   - How to use structured information slots.
   - How to check what the agent handled versus what needs human attention.
   - How to escalate or correct an issue.

2. Product demonstration video
   - Short launch/demo walkthrough for operators.
   - Show the end-to-end value: guest conversation intake, dashboard visibility, QA/intervention, structured data and operational follow-up.
   - Use it for sales, onboarding and internal handoff.

3. Internal support video
   - How Lux Oasis / Conviva team members should operate the tool.
   - What to check daily.
   - What to do when something is missing, wrong or blocked.

## Purpose

- Reduce manual meetings.
- Make onboarding scalable.
- Give clients and team members a single instruction source.
- Help sales by showing the product clearly.
- Make launch support easier for Damiano, Lin and the Conviva team.

## Control Rule

Do not record final public videos until the dashboard flow is stable enough to demo without embarrassing bugs. Before public use, prepare a quick script and demo checklist, then record once the product flow is reliable.
