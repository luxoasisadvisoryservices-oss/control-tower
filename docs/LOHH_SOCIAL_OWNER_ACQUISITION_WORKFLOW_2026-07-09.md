# LOHH Social Owner Acquisition Workflow - 2026-07-09

## Decision

Start social content for Lux Oasis Holiday Homes now.

Do not start public posting for Lux Oasis Advisory & Services LLC yet. Advisory products such as Conviva, Oasis Revenue Lab and Nest Match are not ready enough for public product-led posting. Advisory can prepare the company profile, assets and future content bank only.

## Goal

Use Lux Oasis Holiday Homes social channels to build trust with landlords, owners and agents, and support the property-acquisition lane.

Primary audience:

- Dubai landlords and owners;
- agents with JBR / Palm / Marina owner access;
- people considering holiday-home management;
- operating partner candidates who can bring units.

## Approval Workflow

1. COO agent drafts the content in Telegram or a tracker.
2. Draft includes exact caption, target platform, visual idea/source, audience and business purpose.
3. Damiano approves, edits or rejects.
4. Only approved content is posted or scheduled.
5. Posted items are logged with date, channel, asset, caption and objective.

No public post, ad, DM campaign, revenue claim, guest-data use or owner-facing promise goes live without Damiano approving the exact copy and visual.

## Posting Execution

Near-term, use a semi-automated workflow:

- agent creates the content;
- Damiano approves in Telegram;
- Lin or Damiano posts/schedules through Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn or the native platform;
- agent records the published link/status in Control Tower.

Later automation:

- connect the Facebook Page and Instagram Business account to Meta Business Suite or a Meta developer app;
- keep a posting queue where only approved posts can be published;
- agent can push approved content into the queue, but not publish unapproved content.

## Meta Integration Notes

For full API automation, Instagram must be a Business or Creator account linked to a Facebook Page. Meta publishing also requires the right Page/Instagram permissions and access tokens, and may require app review depending on the posting method and permissions.

Recommendation: do not start with full API automation. Start with approval workflow plus Meta Business Suite/manual scheduling. Add API automation only after the content cadence is proven and the accounts/permissions are stable.
