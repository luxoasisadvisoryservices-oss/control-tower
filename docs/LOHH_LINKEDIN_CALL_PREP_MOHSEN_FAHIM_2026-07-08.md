## Call Structure

Keep each call to 20-25 minutes.

### Opening

Use this positioning:

Lux Oasis Holiday Homes is restructuring around revenue-share / management growth, not heavy fixed-rent subleasing. We are looking for people who can help source, close, onboard and operate suitable Dubai apartments, especially in JBR, Marina, Palm and strong STR areas.

Do not position this as a normal employee role.

### Screen 1 - Real Access

Ask:

- Are you currently active in Dubai holiday homes / short-term rentals?
- Which areas do you know best: JBR, Marina, Palm, Downtown, Business Bay, other?
- Do you personally have owner, landlord, agent, investor, developer or portfolio relationships that could lead to units?
- Can you give examples of units or owner conversations you personally sourced or closed?

Pass signal:

- names real areas, deal types, owner/agent channels and recent examples.

Fail signal:

- speaks only generally about growth, marketing or property management.

### Screen 2 - Deal History

Ask:

- How many holiday-home management contracts, revenue-share units, subleases or owner onboarding deals have you personally closed?
- What was your role: sourcing, negotiation, onboarding, operations, owner reporting?
- What is the typical owner objection and how do you handle it?

Pass signal:

- can explain acquisition mechanics, owner objection handling, expected net/gross economics and onboarding steps.

Fail signal:

- cannot separate STR management, long-term leasing, brokerage and owner reporting.

### Screen 3 - Operating Ability

Ask:

- After a unit is signed, can you help inspect, onboard, coordinate DTCM/building requirements, cleaners, maintenance, photography, OTA launch and owner updates?
- What systems have you used: Hostaway, Guesty, PriceLabs, Airbnb, Booking.com, DTCM?
- What would you need from Lux Oasis to launch the first unit properly?

Pass signal:

- understands practical launch and daily operation.

Fail signal:

- only wants to hand over a lead and disappear.

### Screen 4 - Commercial Fit

Ask:

- Are you looking for salary, referral fee, commission, profit-share, or partnership upside?
- Would you be open to a staged proof model where upside is tied only to units you personally help source/close/operate?
- Are you comfortable with payout based on Lux Oasis retained net profit after owner payout and direct unit costs, not gross booking revenue?

Pass signal:

- open to staged proof and performance upside.

Fail signal:

- wants fixed salary/equity upfront or commission on gross revenue without responsibility.
