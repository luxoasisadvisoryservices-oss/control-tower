# LUX OASIS HOLIDAY HOMES

# Partnership Proposal

## Property Acquisition, Operations & Freelancer Finder Options

Prepared for: [Candidate Name]     |     Date: [Date]

This is a commercial proposal for discussion. It sets out the different ways we can work together to grow the Lux Oasis Holiday Homes managed-property portfolio. It is not an employment offer letter, a broker agreement, a visa offer or a binding contract. A formal agreement follows once terms are agreed and the correct legal or work-authorisation route is confirmed.

## 1. The Opportunity

Lux Oasis Holiday Homes is scaling its managed-property portfolio in Dubai through revenue-share and selected hybrid agreements with property owners. We are looking for commercially capable people who can help us source, sign and operate short-term rental units under our brand, starting in JBR and Palm Jumeirah.

There are two possible routes:

- **Option A: Acquisition & Operations Partner** - for someone who wants to build a portfolio inside Lux Oasis, help close owners, support onboarding and potentially operate units day to day.
- **Option B: Freelancer Property Finder** - for someone who does not want salary, visa sponsorship, employment or operations, but can introduce suitable property owners, landlords, agents or investors.

The right structure depends on the role you actually want to play. Higher upside applies when you take more responsibility. A lighter freelancer route has simpler upside and no employment structure.

## 2. Commercial Principle

All upside is based on **Lux Oasis retained net profit** from the specific units you personally introduce, bring to signature, onboard or operate.

Lux Oasis retained net profit means the Lux Oasis share after:

- owner payout;
- direct unit costs;
- OTA/platform adjustments;
- refunds, chargebacks and revenue adjustments;
- agreed unit-specific operating costs; and
- collected revenue only.

Your upside is not calculated on gross booking revenue, owner profit, full property profit before owner split, company-level profit, unrelated units, uncollected revenue, deposits, reimbursements or pass-through charges.

## 3. Option A - Acquisition & Operations Partner

This route is for someone who wants to build a portfolio inside Lux Oasis. It can include sourcing owners, bringing units to signature, supporting onboarding, managing owner relationships and actively operating properties.

To be clear from the outset: this is a commercial partnership, not a shareholding. There are no company shares and your name is not on the company licence. Where employment or work authorisation is required, the relationship is formalised through a proper compliant structure.

| Your Role on the Unit | What It Means | Your Share |
|---|---|---|
| Acquisition | You source and introduce the owner, bring the unit to signature, and manage the landlord relationship through to handover, without running daily operations. | 18% for 24 months from launch |
| Acquisition + Operations | You source, help close, support onboarding, and actively manage the day-to-day operation and owner relationship for the unit. | 25% ongoing while you operate |
| Full A-to-Z Management | Proven capability managing multiple units end to end with minimal involvement from us, maintaining guest, owner and revenue quality. | Up to 30% |

The 30% level is earned once you are managing multiple units to a consistently high standard. It reflects genuine, proven end-to-end performance rather than a starting position.

### How Option A Shares Apply Over Time

- Acquisition-only share applies for 24 months from the unit going live, then expires.
- Acquisition + Operations share applies on an ongoing basis for as long as you actively operate the unit.
- If you stop operating a unit, the share on that unit returns to the 18% acquisition level.
- If the partnership ends, shares on units still live and generating return to the 18% acquisition level for 3 months from the date of departure, and then stop.

### Launch Advance

Because earnings begin once units are live and generating collected revenue, Lux Oasis may provide a per-unit advance when a unit goes live, to support you through the ramp-up period. This advance is recovered from your first profit-share payouts on that unit. Details are set out in the final agreement.

## 4. Option B - Freelancer Property Finder

This route is for people who can introduce suitable property opportunities but do not want salary, visa sponsorship, employment, daily operations or a long-term role inside Lux Oasis.

For qualified freelancer introductions, the standard structure is:

**15% of Lux Oasis retained net profit for 12 months from the unit launch date.**

For prime-location opportunities, the freelancer commission can increase to:

**18% of Lux Oasis retained net profit for 12 months from the unit launch date.**

The prime-location rate applies to suitable properties in:

- Palm Jumeirah;
- JBR;
- Downtown Dubai.

For selected high-quality introductions, Lux Oasis may agree a longer commission period case by case where the opportunity is strong, direct and commercially attractive.

This gives freelancers a clear way to earn from properties they introduce, without needing to become employees, take a salary, move under our visa, or manage daily operations.

### When Freelancer Commission Starts

Commission starts only when all of the following have happened:

- the freelancer introduced a new eligible property or owner route to Lux Oasis;
- Lux Oasis accepted the introduction in writing;
- the owner/property was not already active in the Lux Oasis pipeline;
- Lux Oasis signed the property under an approved structure;
- the unit launched and started generating collected revenue; and
- the freelancer complied with the agreed introduction and conduct rules.

No commission is due merely for sending a name, link, screenshot, WhatsApp number, portal listing or agent contact unless that route directly leads to a signed and live Lux Oasis unit.

### Freelancer Boundaries

The freelancer does not have authority to:

- represent Lux Oasis as an employee;
- promise income, occupancy, nightly rates or returns;
- agree owner payouts or contract terms;
- sign anything on behalf of Lux Oasis;
- collect money;
- give legal, brokerage, tax or investment advice;
- publish or market a unit under Lux Oasis without written approval.

## 5. Focus Areas

The primary territory is Palm Jumeirah, JBR and Downtown Dubai. Dubai Marina may be considered selectively where the property and owner relationship are strong.

Ideal opportunities include:

- furnished or furnishable 1-2 bedroom apartments;
- strong short-term rental locations;
- owners open to revenue-share or approved hybrid structures;
- realistic launch timing;
- properties where projected revenue can support the owner, Lux Oasis and any approved commission.

## 6. What Lux Oasis Provides

Lux Oasis provides:

- the DET holiday-home licence and operating framework;
- the Lux Oasis Holiday Homes brand and owner-facing credibility;
- pricing and revenue-management systems;
- guest communication systems and standards;
- owner reporting templates and payment processing;
- operational SOPs, listing/channel setup and admin support;
- final approval on whether a property is accepted.

## 7. Responsibilities And Escalation

Depending on the selected route, the role may cover:

- sourcing owners, landlords, agents and investors;
- opening qualified conversations in priority areas;
- helping move suitable units to signature;
- coordinating onboarding and guest-readiness where assigned;
- managing owner relationships where assigned;
- quality control and timely escalation of anything significant;
- weekly pipeline and unit-performance updates where applicable.

Certain decisions are always escalated to Lux Oasis before action: anything involving owner payouts or contract terms, legal or licensing matters, work authorisation, significant guest issues, pricing issues, owner-retention risk or public promises.

## 8. Proof Period

For the Acquisition & Operations Partner route, including candidates moving toward a full-time role, we begin with a 60-day proof period before long-term commitment on either side.

The goal during the proof period:

- open real, qualified owner and agent conversations in JBR and Palm;
- sign 2 to 3 units under Lux Oasis;
- get at least 1 unit live or into onboarding;
- confirm the working relationship, reporting discipline and operating fit.

Strong performance during this 60-day period accelerates everything that follows. If the proof period goes well and both sides want to continue, we can move to the longer-term structure, including compliant work authorisation where required.

For the Freelancer Property Finder route, there is no salary, visa or employment proof period. The freelancer is assessed lead by lead.

## 9. Work Authorisation And Compliance

All work is carried out under the correct legal route. The exact structure depends on the person, activity and current status.

No one should perform work in a way that breaches UAE law, real estate brokerage rules, employment rules, visa rules, platform rules or owner confidentiality. If any activity requires a licence, permit, freelance permission, broker status or specific legal route, that must be confirmed before the activity is carried out.

## 10. Protecting The Relationship

The final agreement will include standard, reasonable terms covering confidentiality of owner and business information, non-circumvention of owners, landlords, agents and units introduced through Lux Oasis, and clear rules on accepted introductions.

These terms are limited and fair. They are focused on protecting the specific relationships and units connected to Lux Oasis. They do not restrict someone from working in the wider industry after the relationship ends.
