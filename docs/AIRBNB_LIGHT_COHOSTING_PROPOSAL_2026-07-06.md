## Important Airbnb Reality Check

Airbnb's current public Co-Host Network pages say the network is available only in selected countries. The public FAQ list includes Australia, Brazil, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Mexico, South Korea, Spain, the UK and the US. It does not list the UAE in that public FAQ.

4. screenshot before any submission;

## Official Airbnb Points To Respect

From Airbnb official pages:

- Co-Host Network eligibility requires a strong Airbnb track record, good standing, verified identity, average rating around 4.8+, and recent hosting/co-hosting volume.
- Airbnb says the host/listing owner controls co-host payouts; co-hosts cannot set their own payouts.
- Co-hosts can support services such as listing setup, pricing and availability, reservations, guest messaging, onsite support, cleaning, photography, interior design, licensing and permits.
- Permissions matter: do not ask for full access unless the work actually needs it.
- Legal/tax/payout requirements may apply.

## Offer Shape

### Package 1 - Listing Tune-Up

Best for hosts who already manage their unit but know the listing is weak.

Services:

- title and description rewrite;
- photo order / gallery notes;
- amenity and house-rule review;
- guest-message template improvement;
- pricing/calendar settings review;
- quick Booking.com/Airbnb visibility and bookability notes where data is available.

Pricing idea:

- one-time AED 500-1,500 depending on depth, or
- free first teardown as lead magnet, then paid implementation.

Low effort:

- mostly analysis and copy;
- no guest operations;
- no cleaning;
- no check-in handling.

### Package 2 - Revenue + Listing Co-Host Lite

Best for hosts who want ongoing help but already have cleaner/check-in/maintenance covered.

Services:

- weekly price/calendar review;
- monthly performance note;
- listing optimisation and promotion suggestions;
- booking gap analysis;
- owner/host action list;
- optional Airbnb co-host access with limited permissions if useful and approved.

Pricing idea:

- 5-10% of Airbnb revenue where co-host payout is supported and the host sets it up; or
- AED 300-750/month per listing; or
- hybrid: setup fee plus small monthly retainer.

Low effort:

- no physical operations by default;
- no 24/7 guest operations unless separately priced;
- no maintenance/cleaning coordination unless the host has local vendors and the scope is written.

### Package 3 - Dubai Physical Co-Host / Management Lead

Best only when the property is in a Lux Oasis operating area or can become a real Lux Oasis unit.

Services:

- full Lux Oasis Holiday Homes management or revenue-share proposal;
- cleaning/check-in/guest operations;
- DTCM and owner reporting.

Pricing:

- use Lux Oasis Holiday Homes management/revenue-share structure, not the light remote package.

Low effort:

- not low effort. Treat as acquisition lead for Lux Oasis Holiday Homes, not the default co-host lite offer.
