# ORL Consultancy-First Pivot - 2026-07-08

Decision: ORL should pivot commercial execution to consultancy first, AI layer second.

Do not sell ORL as a standalone AI product competing with PriceLabs. Sell Damiano-led revenue-management consultancy for STR operators, supported by ORL.

## Why

PriceLabs already owns pricing software, API/MCP, listing metrics, no-booking diagnosis, report builder and pricing/write actions. ORL should not try to match PriceLabs feature-for-feature or price-for-price.

ORL can win by selling Damiano's operator judgement, Dubai STR experience, owner-facing explanation and implementation follow-through, with AI as the delivery engine.

## Offer Ladder

1. Revenue Health Check: one-listing or small-portfolio review, revenue leakage notes, OTA/listing diagnosis, top 5 actions and 30-day plan.
2. Revenue Manager Plus: recurring consultancy retainer with weekly pricing/listing review, monthly owner report, recommendation ledger and implementation check.
3. ORL Pro / Scenario Desk: premium owner-ready scenario plans with visible assumptions and sensitivity.

## Immediate Execution

1. Finish Section A `RevenueState` fix.
2. Prepare consultancy-first landing page section.
3. Create one-listing `Revenue Health Check` template.
4. Use Oasis2 / Wellspring style proof.
5. Build small Dubai STR operator prospect list.
6. Offer manual/consultative review first.
7. Use ORL internally to produce reports and recommendations.
8. Expose client AI only after it is stable.

Positioning:

> ORL is not a PriceLabs competitor. It is a revenue-management consultancy layer that uses PriceLabs, PMS data and OTA diagnosis to turn pricing data into owner-ready decisions and implementation.
