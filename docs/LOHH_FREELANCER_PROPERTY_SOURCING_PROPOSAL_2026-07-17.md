# Property Sourcing Freelancer Proposal

Status: Commercial proposal for discussion only

This proposal is for people who can introduce suitable property owners, landlords, agents or investors to Lux Oasis Holiday Homes LLC, but who do not want a salary, employment, visa sponsorship or an operating role inside the company.

It is separate from the Lux Oasis acquisition and operations partner proposal. That fuller proposal is for people who want to source, close, onboard and operate properties under a more active commercial structure.

## 1. The Opportunity

Lux Oasis Holiday Homes is growing its Dubai short-term rental portfolio through revenue-share and selected hybrid structures with property owners.

We are looking for freelancers and commercial introducers who can help us find suitable apartments and owner relationships, especially in:

- JBR
- Palm Jumeirah
- Dubai Marina, selectively
- Downtown Dubai, only where the property economics are clearly strong

The role is simple: introduce qualified property opportunities to Lux Oasis. If the property signs with Lux Oasis, launches, and generates collected revenue, you earn a commission from the Lux Oasis retained net profit from that unit.

## 2. Recommended Freelancer Commission

For a pure freelancer / finder role, the recommended structure is:

**15% of Lux Oasis retained net profit for 12 months from the unit launch date.**

This is the best default because it gives the freelancer real upside while keeping Lux Oasis protected. The freelancer is not taking salary risk, visa responsibility, owner-retention responsibility, guest operations, daily maintenance, pricing, reporting or compliance workload.

For exceptional cases, Lux Oasis may extend the commission period to **18 months**, but only when:

- the lead is a direct owner or unusually strong owner route;
- the opportunity was not already known to Lux Oasis;
- the property is in a priority area;
- the freelancer materially helps move the owner to signature; and
- the final unit economics support the longer commission period.

The default should remain 12 months. Do not offer 24 months for a pure finder unless there is a very strong strategic reason, because that is too close to the acquisition-partner structure without the same operating responsibility.

## 3. How Commission Is Calculated

Commission is calculated only on **Lux Oasis retained net profit** from the specific unit the freelancer personally introduces.

Lux Oasis retained net profit means the Lux Oasis share after:

- owner payout;
- direct unit costs;
- OTA/platform adjustments;
- refunds, chargebacks and revenue adjustments;
- agreed unit-specific operating costs; and
- collected revenue only.

Commission is not calculated on:

- gross booking revenue;
- owner profit;
- the full property profit before owner split;
- unrelated Lux Oasis units;
- company-level profit;
- uncollected revenue;
- deposits, reimbursements, pass-through charges or owner funds.

## 4. When Commission Starts

Commission starts only when all of the following have happened:

- the freelancer introduced a new eligible property or owner route to Lux Oasis;
- Lux Oasis accepted the introduction in writing;
- the owner/property was not already active in the Lux Oasis pipeline;
- Lux Oasis signed the property under an approved structure;
- the unit launched and started generating collected revenue; and
- the freelancer has complied with the agreed introduction and conduct rules.

No commission is due merely for sending a name, link, screenshot, WhatsApp number, portal listing or agent contact unless that route directly leads to a signed and live Lux Oasis unit.

## 5. What The Freelancer Does

The freelancer's role may include:

- identifying suitable owners, landlords, agents and investors;
- making an introduction to Lux Oasis;
- helping Lux Oasis understand the owner, property and opportunity;
- supporting the first call or meeting where useful;
- helping collect basic information, such as location, unit type, asking terms and owner expectations;
- following up with the contact until Lux Oasis takes over the commercial discussion.

The freelancer does not have authority to:

- represent Lux Oasis as an employee;
- promise income, occupancy, nightly rates or returns;
- agree owner payouts or contract terms;
- sign anything on behalf of Lux Oasis;
- collect money;
- give legal, brokerage, tax or investment advice;
- publish or market a unit under Lux Oasis without written approval.

## 6. What Lux Oasis Provides

Lux Oasis provides:

- the Lux Oasis Holiday Homes brand;
- DET holiday-home licence and operating framework;
- owner-facing proposal and commercial discussion;
- pricing and revenue-management approach;
- guest communication and operational systems;
- onboarding, listing and channel setup;
- owner reporting and payout process;
- final decision on whether the property is accepted.

## 7. Eligible Properties

Lux Oasis is mainly looking for:

- furnished or furnishable 1-2 bedroom apartments;
- properties in strong short-term rental locations;
- owners open to revenue-share or approved hybrid structures;
- units with realistic launch timing;
- properties where projected revenue can support the owner, Lux Oasis and any freelancer commission.

Priority areas are JBR and Palm Jumeirah. Other areas may be considered only if the property economics and owner relationship are strong.

## 8. Lead Protection And Expiry

A freelancer's introduction is protected only if Lux Oasis confirms it as accepted in writing.

Recommended protection period:

- 6 months from accepted introduction to signed agreement.

If the owner/property does not sign within 6 months, the introduction expires unless Lux Oasis agrees in writing to extend it.

If multiple people introduce the same property or owner, Lux Oasis will decide the recognised introducer based on who created the first useful, documented and accepted route.

## 9. Compliance And Status

This is a freelancer / commercial introduction proposal only. It is not an employment offer, visa offer, broker appointment, agency appointment or company partnership.

The freelancer is responsible for making sure they are allowed to perform any freelance, referral, brokerage or introduction activity they undertake. If any activity requires a licence, permit or specific legal route, that must be handled before the activity is carried out.

No work should be done in a way that breaches UAE law, real estate brokerage rules, employment rules, visa rules, platform rules or owner confidentiality.

Use this as the default:

- **15% for 12 months** for pure introductions.
- **15% for up to 18 months** only for exceptional direct-owner routes or unusually strong properties.
- Keep **18-25%+** for people who are doing more than freelance sourcing: close support, onboarding, owner management and operations.

This lets Lux Oasis widen the LinkedIn search without overpaying passive introducers or weakening the economics needed to reach the AED 50,000 monthly operating-profit target.
